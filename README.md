# Probability Puzzles

A collection of programming solutions and simulations for various probability puzzles, ranging from classic problems to modern brain teasers.

## **Classical Problems (Frederick Mosteller)**
Many of these solutions are based on problems from [Fifty Challenging Problems in Probability](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00A3M0VV8) by Frederick Mosteller.

### **Dice & Coin Puzzles**
* [Isaac Newton Helps Samuel Pepys](scripts/newton_helps_pepys.py) (Problem 18) - Which of three events is more likely when throwing dice?
* [Trials Until First Success](scripts/trials_until_success.py) (Problem 4) - On average, how many times must a die be thrown until one rolls a six?
* [An Even Split at Coin Tossing](scripts/even_split.py) (Problem 17) - When 100 coins are tossed, what is the probability that exactly 50 are heads?

### **Card Puzzles**
* [The First Ace](scripts/first_ace.py) (Problem 40) - How many cards are dealt from a shuffled deck before the first ace appears?
* [Average Number of Matches](scripts/average_number_of_matches.py) (Problem 43) - What is the average number of matches between two decks of shuffled cards?

### **Gambling & Betting Strategies**
* [Chuck-a-Luck](scripts/chuck_a_luck.py) (Problem 19) - What is the player's expected loss per unit stake in Chuck-a-Luck?
* [Craps](scripts/craps.py) (Problem 20) - What is the player's chance to win at Craps?
* [Curing the Compulsive Gambler](scripts/curing_the_compulsive_gambler.py) (Problem 21) - Will a player be behind after 36 plays of one number in Roulette?
* [Gambler's Ruin](scripts/gamblers_ruin.py) (Problem 24) - What is the probability of a player winning under specific conditions?
* [Bold Play vs. Cautious Play](scripts/bold_play_vs_cautious_play.py) (Problem 25) - Compare the merits of two different gambling strategies.

### **Geometry & Continuous Probability**
* [Lengths of Random Chords](scripts/length_of_random_chords.py) (Problem 26) - What is the probability that a random chord on a circle exceeds its radius?
* [The Clumsy Chemist](scripts/clumsy_chemist.py) (Problem 28) - What is the average length of pieces from randomly broken glass rods?
* [The Little End of the Stick](scripts/little_end_of_the_stick.py) (Problem 30) - What is the average length of the smaller piece of a randomly broken stick?
* [The Broken Bar](scripts/broken_bar.py) (Problem 31) - What are the average sizes of all pieces of a bar randomly broken in three?

### **Combinatorics & Arrangements**
* [Collecting Coupons](scripts/collecting_coupons.py) (Problem 14) - How many coupons must you collect to complete a set?
* [The Theater Row](scripts/theater_row.py) (Problem 15) - How many adjacent "marriageable couples" are expected in a random row of 15?

### **Logic, Timing & Decisions**
* [Successive Wins](scripts/successive_wins.py) (Problem 2) - Which series of games offers a better probability of winning two in a row?
* [The Flippant Juror](scripts/flippant_juror.py) (Problem 3) - Which jury has a better probability of making the correct decision?
* [Catching the Cautious Counterfeiter](scripts/catching_the_cautious_counterfeiter.py) (Problem 23) - What is the probability of catching a counterfeiter during sampling?
* [The Hurried Duelers](scripts/hurried_duelers.py) (Problem 27) - If duelists arrive at random times, what fraction of duels lead to violence?
* [Will Second-Best Be Runner-Up?](scripts/second_best.py) (Problem 10) - What is the probability that the second-best player is the runner-up in a tournament?
* [Twin Knights](scripts/twin_knights.py) (Problem 11) - What are the odds that twins will meet in a tournament?

## **Miscellaneous Puzzles**
Problems from various sources including FiveThirtyEight, Futility Closet, and Twitter.

### **Games & Gambling**
* [The Petersburg Paradox](scripts/petersburg_paradox.py) - What would be a fair price to pay the casino for entering the game?
* [Yahtzee](scripts/yahtzee.py) - What is the probability of rolling five of a kind with three rolls?
* [Carnival Barker](scripts/carnival_barker.py) - What is the expected value of winnings in a specific carnival dice game?
* [Three Flips](scripts/three_flips.py) - Should you pay to play a specific coin-flipping game with tiered payouts?
* [Flipping Four Coins](scripts/four_flips.py) - Evaluating a bet on whether heads and tails both appear twice in four flips.

### **Dice & Coin Sequences**
* [Coin Flip Sequences](scripts/coin_flip_sequences.py) - Which sequence takes longer on average: HTH or HTT?
* [A Dicey Question](scripts/dicey_question.py) - Is it more likely to roll exactly one six or no sixes in five dice?
* [Flipping 100 Coins](scripts/flipping_n_coins.py) - How many rounds until only one coin remains when re-flipping heads?
* [Chance of Dying](scripts/chance_of_dying.py) - What are the chances of rolling a sum of 57 or greater with 19 dice?

### **Arrangements & Seating**
* [All Aboard](scripts/all_aboard.py) - What’s the probability that the last passenger on an airplane finds his seat occupied?
* [Theater Seats](scripts/theater_seats.py) - What is the probability that two friends won't sit together in a cinema?
* [Gummy Bears](scripts/gummy_bears.py) - What is the probability of selecting five different flavors from a pack?
* [Infinite Monkeys](scripts/infinite_monkeys.py) - Probability of specific names appearing if monkeys type letters at random.
* [Minority Selection](scripts/minority_selection.py) - Probability that no member of a minority party is selected for N slots.
* [Bags of Marbles](scripts/bags_of_marbles.py) - Probability that a second marble drawn is white in a specific game.
* [A Painting Puzzle](scripts/painting_puzzle.py) - What is the expected number of turns for all balls to become the same color?

### **Data & Bayesian Analysis**
* [Hit and Run](scripts/hit_and_run.py) - Bayesian probability of a cab's color involved in an accident.
* [State Names Letter Frequency](scripts/state_names.py) - Analysis of letter frequencies in US state names.

### **Miscellaneous Logic**
* [Lunch in the Park](scripts/lunch_in_the_park.py) - What is the probability that two friends meet for lunch given random arrivals?
* [Missiles](scripts/missiles.py) - Probability that a target is hit by at least one of four missiles.
* [Quiz Answers](scripts/quiz_answers.py) - Generating an optimal strategy for a specific quiz game.
* [Draw Two](scripts/draw_two.py) - Expected value of the sum of two integers drawn from 1-10.
* [Exceed 1](scripts/exceed_1.py) - How many random numbers are needed to sum to more than 1?
* [How Long Will You Shuffle This Damn Deck Of Cards](scripts/specific_shuffling_problem.py) - Reversals required in a specific card shuffling problem.
* [Lonely Card Game](scripts/lonely_card_game.py) - Probability of winning a game where you lose on a rank match during deal.
